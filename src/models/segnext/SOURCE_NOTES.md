## Role in This Lab

`segnext` is kept as a modern semantic segmentation reference model based on convolutional attention and MSCAN. It can be used as:

- a stronger non-DeepLab baseline,
- a convolutional attention comparison against CBAM/PPM variants,
- a candidate architecture for future grape disease segmentation experiments.
